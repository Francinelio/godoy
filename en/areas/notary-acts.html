Lawyer's Notary Acts

With legal grounds in Decree Law no. 28/2000 of March 13, c / c Decree Law no. 237/2001 of August 30, c / c art. 38 of Decree Law no. 76-A / 2006 of March 29, and c / c Decree Law no. 116/2008 of July 4, c / c Decree Law 125/2006 of June 29, c / c Ordinance No. 1535/2008, of December 30, as amended by Ordinance No. 286/2012, of 20 of September, c / c Regulation of Registries and Notaries, approved by Decree-Law No. 322-A / 2001, of 14 December - Ordinance 574/2008 of 4 July - Ordinance 385/2004 of 16 April, c / c, subparagraphs "a" and "d" of Article 3 (1) of the Notary Code, conditions were created for lawyers to provide services related to all such acts performed with the same force and the same probative value as similar acts. notary, in the following related services:

Forensic purposes, recognitions of signatures and photocopy certifications (by similar or personally)

Translation certifications

Authentications of terms and private documents

Single contracts:

  • Buy and sell;
  • Shares;
  • Donation;
  • Giving of compliance;
  • Division of common thing;
  • Confession of debt;
  • Declaration of Consent.

Online records:

  • Building;
  • Commercial;
  • Automotive;

Registrations by transcription

  • (It consists in extracting the elements that define the legal situation of the entities subject to registration contained in the documents presented).

Deposit records

  • (It consists in the mere archiving of documents that title facts subject to registration).

Trade records:

  • Constitution of a commercial company;
  • The unification, division and transfer of quotas of companies by quotas;
  • The constitution and transfer of usufruct, the pledge, seizure, listing and attachment of quotas or rights over them;
  • The appointment and termination of office, for any reason other than the course of time, of the members of the company's administrative and supervisory bodies, as well as of the company secretary;
  • The change of headquarters of the company;
  • The extension, merger, split, transformation and dissolution of companies;
  • The capital increase and any other amendment to the articles of association.
